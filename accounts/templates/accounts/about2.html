What inspired us?

We are dedicated to fighting malnutrition in children under the age of five, particularly in rural areas. We prioritize offering affordable and accessible meal plans to ensure that these children receive the nutrition they need to thrive. We also assess mothers and provide them with nutritionally balanced foods and recipes that are locally available.

By promoting natural alternatives to artificial nutritious foods, we aim to instill healthy eating habits and give children the best possible start in life. Our mission is to eradicate malnutrition by making nutritious meals readily available and empowering mothers to make informed choices for their children's well-being.
